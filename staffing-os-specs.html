04 · security & compliance

Trust is the product. So it's built in.

⛭Role-based access

Staffing OS credentials map to your ATS roles — recruiter, sales, ops, leadership — so only the right fields are ever exposed or writable.

⚿OAuth & encryption

Secure OAuth into the ATS. Data encrypted in transit and at rest. No credentials stored in prompts, ever.

✓Consent & right-to-represent

Candidates grant and revoke representation per firm and per role. Every submittal carries e-signed consent.

◷Candidate data ownership

Candidates can view, export, and revoke their data anytime — GDPR/consent by design, not bolted on.
